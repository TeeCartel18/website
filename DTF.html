About DTF Printing

DTF stands for Direct-to-Film printing – a revolutionary garment printing method that allows for vibrant, detailed designs to be printed onto a special film and then transferred onto a wide range of fabrics.

This method ensures your prints are not only high-quality but also durable and flexible. It works great on cotton, polyester, blends, and even leather!

Why Choose DTF?

  • ✅ Bright and vibrant colors
  • ✅ Works on all fabric types
  • ✅ Long-lasting and stretch-resistant
  • ✅ Cost-effective for small batches

Whether you’re printing custom t-shirts, hoodies, baby onesies, or hats – DTF gives you the professional finish you're after.
