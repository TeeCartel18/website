How DTF Printing Works

Step 1: Create Your Design

Send us your custom artwork or let our design team help you create something unique. We accept all major file formats.

Step 2: Print on Film

We print your design in full color on special DTF transfer film using high-resolution printers and vibrant inks.

Step 3: Apply Adhesive

A powdered adhesive is applied to the print, then melted to ensure strong bonding with the garment.

Step 4: Transfer to Garment

The printed film is heat-pressed onto your chosen garment. The result? A bright, detailed, and durable design that feels great.
